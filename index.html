How to Use the App

Instructions:

1. When you open the app, you will see a text field where you need to enter your ID number.

2. Enter your real ID number as registered with Home Affairs. You'll be redirected to the registration screen.

3. On the registration screen, input your details as they are recorded with Home Affairs. If the details are incorrect, the app will display an "Invalid" message.

4. If you're testing, click the "Get Testing Details" button to be redirected to a webpage where dummy details can be generated.

5. Use the generated ID number, names, surname, and gender to test the app.

6. After entering the details and passing validation, you can create a 6-digit PIN and confirm it.

7. You'll then be directed to the home page, where you can view news (scrollable and clickable) and a slideshow showing the top 5 results in different categories.

8. The voting button will only be active during voting periods. To vote, select your province and candidates. You can only vote once.

9. You can exit the voting screen by clicking the back button.

10. To view results, click on the results screen.

11. Swipe from any screen or click the menu button on the top-left corner to access other options like settings, profile, and sign out.

12. In settings, you can delete your account, view notifications from admins, or send help requests if you're facing issues. Admins can respond to these requests.
